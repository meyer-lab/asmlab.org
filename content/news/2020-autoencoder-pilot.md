title: New funding
date: 2020-08-16 12:00:00
---
The lab has been awarded a pilot grant from the NCI to combine mechanistic models and AI in the form of autoencoders to model patient-specific phosphoproteomic changes. This is in collaboration with [Emek Demir at OHSU](https://www.ohsu.edu/people/emek-demir-phd), [Sara Gosline at PNNL](https://saragosline.wordpress.com), and [Fabian Fröhlich at Harvard](https://scholar.harvard.edu/fabianfroehlich/home).
