Meyer Lab at UCLA

We apply experimental and computational strategies to measure, model, and then therapeutically manipulate cell-to-cell communication, with applications in the development of immune and cancer therapies.

Opportunities

We welcome applications from qualified candidates at all levels. Given the high volume of inquiries we receive and the limitations of email communication in fully understanding candidates' interests and potential fit with our lab, we encourage interested individuals to schedule a personal consultation with Dr. Meyer [here](https://calendar.app.google/1iYzSAq2Mrjd5i2f6). Please note that we are unable to respond to individual email inquiries about positions.
